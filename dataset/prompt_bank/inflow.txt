The Bike data set is the bicycle demand data of 295 traffic nodes in New York, and the bicycle demand usually refers to the bicycle use in the region. The data contains the time series data of 290 nodes, and the data demand information is aggregated at the one-hour level. Annual data from 2023-01-01 00:00:00 to 2024-01-01 23:00:00, in which the demand for day and night shows certain seasonal and cyclical changes, in addition, the demand data includes the time series data of nodes for bicycle inflow and outflow to the site, Each data node contains the target value "bicycle demand", where the training set, verification set, and test set are divided into 0.7, 0.1, and 0.2.
